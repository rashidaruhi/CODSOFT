ABOUT DECCAN HIGH SCHOOL


Deccan High School is a prestigious instituion located at the heart of the Asbestos Colony Community . Founded in 2004 , our school has a rich history of academic excellence and community involvement.


OUR VISION AND MISSION

At Deccan High School, our mission is to nurture students to become responsible global citizens through a culture of lifelong learning, critical thinking, and creativity. We envision an innovative educational environment where every student is empowered to reach their full potential and contribute positively to society.


ACADEMIC AND EXTRACURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

Deccan High School excels in providing a balanced education. Our rigorous academics include AP and honors courses, complemented by diverse extracurriculars such as student clubs, sports teams, and award-winning arts programs. This blend ensures students are well-prepared for future success.

Meet Our Prinicpal

Shaik Khazim, with over 20 years of experience and an M.Sc. and B.Ed. from Osmania University, is dedicated to student-centered learning and innovation. He has received The Best Teacher's Award from The Balanagar Mandal Recognized Private Schools Correspondents Association and has been honored by Chandrababu Naidu.

Age Criteria

  • -> To get an admission for your child for Nursery, he/she must be 2 1/2 years old as on 1st April of the academic year. To get an admission for your child for LKG, he/she must be 4 years old as on 1st April of the academic year.
  • -> Students seeking admission to classes UKG-X, concept check will be conducted.
  • -> Admissions will be based on their aptitude.

Documents Required For Admission

  • 1. Date of Birth copy of the child.
  • 2. Aadhar copy of the child.
  • 3. Caste certificate copy of the child.
  • 4. Transfer certificate from the previous school.
  • 5. Bonafide from the previous school.
  • 6. Passport size color Photographs of the child and parents.
